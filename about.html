About Me

I'm a designer and creative technologist focused on digital accessibility, UX research, and fabrication. I have a passion for design that centers inclusion and curiosity, with accessibility, iterating, and experimentation being at the core of my work.

I earned my undergraduate degree from NYU's College of Arts & Science, majoring in Psychology with minors in Web Development and Integrated Design & Media, and will be attending NYU Tisch's Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP) for my MPS.

My skills include Figma, user research, prototyping, physical computing, fabrication, and visual design. I'm currently open to all forms of work, collaboration, or opportunities.

Outside of design, I serve in a variety of capacities, as a production sound mixer, classical composer, pianist, handbell ringer, and many more! My areas of creative ability expand outside of design, where I draw most of my inspiration from!
