"Brown Sugar Brilliance"

Role: Marketing Coordinator/Social Media
Tools: Illustrator, After Effects, Canva

Brown Sugar Brilliance is a NYU club that started in 2024. I had the amazing opportunity of serving as its first graphic designer and marketing lead.

Logo

Visual Foundation Studio is a class in the NYU Tandon School of Engineering's Integrated Design & Media Department that is fundamental to understanding visual designing elements such as color theory, typography, and page layout amongst many others. The class swept through many features of Adobe CS and Figma. My class process and portfolio can be found here. The Figma prototype for the final project can be found here.

Design Samples

As a means of commemorating my final Fall Semester at NYU and to produce my final project for my Contemporary Photography Techniques class, I photographed and edited a magazine showcasing comical aspects of my friends lives. The magazine was shot on a Sony Alpha 7 III and my iPhone 12 across 4 days, with over 2,000 photos, and edited within two days with Photoshop and InDesign. The editing was purposely made to invoke an older camera that has a tint to recreate nostalgia.
